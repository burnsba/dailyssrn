About

SSRN is a great resource, but the options for RSS subscriptions are pretty limited -- by author, top papers, or even by publication, but only if you create an account.

Here you can subscribe to an RSS feed which will show all papers (authors, title, and abstract) which were updated or revised over the last day. The feed is updated once a day, and there's no signup.
